Eco Champions: Clean Earth Adventure

Eco Champions is a cinematic environmental simulation game where players take action to restore a polluted world and bring life back to the planet.

In this interactive experience, you will:

🌱 Plant trees to fight deforestation
♻️ Convert agricultural waste into eco-friendly briquettes
🌫️ Reduce pollution and improve air quality
🚲 Watch the world transform as people and clean transport return Gameplay Features
Dynamic environment that changes based on your actions
Tree planting system with visual growth
Briquette production using agricultural waste
Animated citizens, bikes, and pollution effects
Cinematic victory system with celebration effects
 Impact & Purpose
This game is designed to raise awareness about environmental sustainability and climate change solutions.

It supports the following United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

SDG 13 – Climate Action
SDG 15 – Life on Land
SDG 7 – Affordable and Clean Energy
SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production
